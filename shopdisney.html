ShopDisney.com and the Disney store is the best place to look for authentic Disney items. You can find things ranging from collectible items to clothing. They even sell some collaboration items such as the Colourpop x Disney (makeup) and Kipling x Disney (purses).

Every once in a while you will see Disney Parks items. These are items that are made especially for the Disney Parks (Disneyland, Walt Disney World, etc.) and meant to only be sold in the Disney Parks. This gives you the opportunity to purchase some cool items that you might not have been able to purchase otherwise.
