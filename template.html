Out for Coffee

----



During my second year of college I took a photography class where we had an assignment to go to San Francisco and take 1000 photos. The goal of the assignment was to get used to taking many photos in order to get a few good ones, since they can't all be great.
For breakfast I picked up this coffee in a small coffee shop. This was one of the first pictures I took for the assignment during that trip.
